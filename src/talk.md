## Style Guide

### Structure - The Golden Structure

One of a good structure is the **Golden Structure**.

It's on 3 parts :

- Why 
- How
- What

#### Why

- **Why ?** : Understand the goal

#### How

- **Why ?** : Understand how we can

#### What

- **Why ?** : Understand what to use
